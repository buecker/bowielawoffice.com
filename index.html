Bowie Law Firm

We are a full service Real Estate and U.S. Small Business Administration law firm representing individuals and businesses throughout Texas, including Fortune 500 Companies. We provide SBA loan consultation and closings. We perform commercial and residential title closings, and can prepare lender and seller owner financed loan documents, including “Wrap” and “Subject-To” loan documents. We litigate and represent clients in landlord/tenant disputes and evictions.

Bowie Law Firm is an authorized Fee Office for Capital Title and can provide title closings throughout Texas.

Bowie Law Firm provides free consultation for SBA loans in Texas, New Mexico and Louisiana, and is a SBA designated closing attorney.

Kevin Bowie is a certified Arbitrator for the Better Business Bureau.

About Us

Kevin Bowie
Attorney

Austin native, Kevin Bowie, serves as Managing Shareholder for the Bowie Law Firm. He received his Bachelor’s degree in Political Science from Texas A&M University in 1991.

With over thirteen years of litigation and transactional experience, Kevin has successfully represented both corporate and individual clients in claims involving toxic tort liability, products liability, premises liability and real estate. 

Kevin Bowie has been distinguished with the esteemed title of Fellow in
the Texas Bar Foundation, an accolade among the most prestigious for State
Bar of Texas members. Furthermore, he holds the designation as a
Specialized Small Business Administration (SBA) Closing Attorney and is
certified as an Arbitrator by the Better Business Bureau.

Sofia Nader
Legal Assistant

Sofia Nader serves as an Escrow Officer and Legal Assistant for the Bowie Law Firm. Sofia grew up in the Rio Grande Valley and attended St. Edward’s University in Austin.

Sofia has over seven years of Title, Real Estate, and Legal experience. She is a licensed notary and is fluent in Spanish. Sofia closes residential and commercial purchases and refinances.

Kelli Burnes
Marketing Manager

Kelli Burnes is the Marketing Manager and Escrow Officer for Bowie Law Firm. Kelli is a native Austinite and graduated from The University of Texas with a Bachelor’s of Science degree in Kinesiology in 1994.

Kelli previously worked in the Mortgage industry as an Account Executive, and has over six years’ experience in the title and mortgage industry. She is a licensed notary.

Janet Grundy
Attorney

Janet Grundy has been a licensed Texas Attorney for 38 years. She graduated from Cornell University with a Bachelor’s degree in Biology in 1978, and from Tulane University School of Law in 1981.

Janet’s primary focus has been in both insurance and banking law. She has served as corporate counsel, managing the claims and litigation for several business entities. Janet is a former law clerk to the Honorable Mary Lou Robinson, United States District Judge of the Northern District of Texas.
